# This file explains the variables in the tidy dataset

The tidy dataset created in the project consists of 81 columns, 79 of which represents the mean values of different measurements. 

The first two columns pertain to activity and the subject.

1. The **activity** is one of the six following activities, as specified in the raw dataset:  *WALKING*, *WALKING_UPSTAIRS*, *WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS*, *SITTING*, *STANDING*, *LAYING*.

2. The **subject** is the identifier of a subject (a person in the experiment) and it is an integer from 1 to 30. All other columns (columns 3 to 81) pertain to mean values of different measurements that measured mean or standard deviation of different qunantities in the raw dataset.
